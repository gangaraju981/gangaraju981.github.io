Tandem Plunging Airfoils — Discrete & Lumped Vortex Methods

Python · Course Project

Built a computational framework implementing lumped and discrete vortex methods, validating predictions against Theodorsen analytical theory across reduced frequencies from 0.05 to 1. Decomposed unsteady forces into circulatory and added-mass components to resolve the transition from a drag-producing wake to a thrust-producing reverse von Kármán street.
